Junjie Wang (王俊杰)

Shenzhen, China

dreamboy.gns@sjtu.edu.cn

I am a machine learning engineer in Tencent@IEG. I received my Master’s degree in Electronic Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University(SJTU) in 2024. Before that, I received my B.S. in Computer Science and Technology from SJTU.

When I was a student, I worked on research in 3D human pose estimation, 3D human mesh recovery, object animating and structured illuminational microscopy, under the supervision of Dr. Zhenbo Yu, Prof. Bing Bing Ni, Prof. Xiaoyan Wu. I also participated in virtual avatar projects in MetaCube Lab in SJTU. After graduation (up to now), I have been working on the application of latest machine learning techniques in game industries.

I have broad interests in games and 3D vision tasks (e.g. 3D Generative Models, Motion Capture, Differentible Rendering). I am a beginner but keep learning :).

In my life, I am a metaphysics lover. I love reading and photography.
